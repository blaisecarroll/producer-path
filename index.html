Why I Walked Away From Everything the Industry Told Me About Leads

A few years ago I was at one of the largest Allstate agencies in Utah, and lead generation felt broken. Cold calling was ineffective, burnout was high, and every internet lead we bought was being called by five other agents at the same time. We were paying good money to compete for people who didn't want to hear from any of us.

Then a simple thought changed everything. Insurance is a relationship business, so why were we trying to build relationships with total strangers over the phone? The people we wanted were already gathering in our own community, at events happening every weekend.

So we tested it. We targeted local events where our dream clients would be, used a few specific strategies to start conversations, and almost immediately saw the fruit of our labor. After one community outreach event, pickup rates, close ratio, and retention all improved.

We got obsessed. We searched for more events, refined our scripts, and tightened the system year after year. That agency grew into one of the biggest in the state, all without cold calling. ProducerPath is everything we learned, packaged so you can do the same in your market.
